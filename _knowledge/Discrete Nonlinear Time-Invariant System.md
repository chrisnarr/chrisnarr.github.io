> [!definition] Definition
>
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> 	\underline{x}(k+1) & = \underline{f}(\underline{x}(k),\underline{u}(k)) \\
> 	\underline{y}(k) & = \underline{g}(\underline{x}(k),\underline{u}(k))
> \end{aligned}
> $$
>
> $k \in \mathbb{N}_{0}$ >$x(0) = x_0$ >$x(k) \in \mathbb{R}^{n}$: state
> $u(k) \in \mathbb{R}^{m}$: control input
