> [!idea] Idea
>
> - Shows frequency response of a system
> - Plots the **system's gain** and **phase shift** over **frequency**
> - Gain is usually plotted on a **logarithmic** scale (dB), phase shift is usually plotted on a **linear** scale (deg)
> - Useful for **analyzing** and **designing** control systems, communication systems, and other systems with a frequency-dependent behavior

# Relation

- The [[Bode Diagram]] can be seen as the slice of the [[Pole-Zero Plot]] at the imaginary axis, because it depicts only **real** frequency components (since sinuses are used for frequency sweeps)
