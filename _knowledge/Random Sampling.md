> [!idea] Idea
>
> - Sample **independent** **random** parameter combinations from the entire sample space

![[Random Sampling.gif|500]]

> [!pro] **PRO**
>
> - new sample points are generated without taking into account the previously generated sample points, which enables **parallelization**
> - You don't need to know beforehand how many sample points are needed

> [!con] **CON**
>
> - Assumes **independence** of parameters
> - Often **not representative** for the whole sampling space
