> [!idea] Idea
>
> - Refers to various phenomena that arise when analyzing and organizing data in high-dimensional spaces that do not occur in low-dimensional settings
> - When the dimensionality increases, the volume of the space increases so fast that the available data becomes sparse
> - In order to obtain a reliable result, the amount of data needed often grows exponentially with the dimensionality
