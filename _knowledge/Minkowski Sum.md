- Minkowski sum of two sets of position vectors $A$ and $B$ in Euclidean space is formed by adding each vector in $A$ to each vector in $B$:

$$
A \oplus B = \{ \boldsymbol{a} + \boldsymbol{b} | \boldsymbol{a}\in A, \boldsymbol{b}\in B\}
$$
