In nonlinear programming, we try to find an **minimum** (or maximum) of a **nonlinear** function subject to **equality** and **inequality** constraints.

> [!idea] General NLP Formulation
>
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> \min_w & \phi(w) \\
> \text{s.t. } & g(w) = 0 \\
> & g(w)\geq 0
> \end{aligned}
> $$
