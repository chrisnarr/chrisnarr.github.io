> [!idea] Idea
>
> - shows the frequency response of a system as **vector** in the **complex plane**
> - is generated by plotting the complex transfer function of the system and varying the frequency from zero (or minus infinity) to infinity
> - by analyzing the location of the vector in the complex plane, it is possible to determine the **stability** of the system
