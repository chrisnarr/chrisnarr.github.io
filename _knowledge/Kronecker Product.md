> [!idea] Idea
> The Kronecker Product is an operation on two matrices forming a block matrix. You multiply each element of the first matrix with the whole second matrix.

> [!definition] Definition
> If $A$ is an $m \times n$ matrix and $B$ is an $p \times q$ matrix, then the Kronecker product $A \otimes B$ is the $pm \times qn$ block matrix:
>
> $$
> A \otimes B =
> \begin{bmatrix}
> a_{11}B & \dots & a_{1n}B \\
> \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\
> a_{m1}B & \dots &  a_{mn}B \\
> \end{bmatrix}
> $$
