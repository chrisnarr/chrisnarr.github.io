> [!idea] Idea
>
> - Find the **function** that minimizes another function

- In contrast to Differential Calculus, which tries to find a **point** that minimizes a function

> [!example]- Example: Path Minimization
>
> - Minimize the **distance** of the path connecting two points
> - Minimize the **time** of a ball rolling down a slope (**Brachistochrone Problem**)
> - Minimize the **energy**: Find the shape of a chain hanging from two fixpoints
